FAQ

  • Q1: What should I do if I encounter a blank screen during a conversation?

    If you encounter a blank screen during a conversation, please refresh the page.


  • Q2: What should I do if my session expires?

    You will receive a pop-up message indicating that the session has expired. Click "OK" and re-login again to continue.


  • Q3: What should I do if Hannah is not speaking?

    If Hannah is not speaking, please refresh or re-login again.


  • Q4: What should I do if I don't like any answer by Hannah?

    Please click on the feedback icon and submit your feedback.


  • Q5: What should I do if I need to know how to use the application?

    Click on the info icon on Hannah interaction page or go to the help page.


  • Q6: What should I do if I am unable to log in to the application because my account has expired?

    Please contact your administrator or email at support@edyou.com for assistance.


  • Q7: What should I do if the presentation doesn't load?

    Please refresh the page or close the learning module and restart the presentation again.


  • Q8: What if I'm not able to see Hannah on screen?

    If you are not able to see Hannah on screen just refresh the page once.


  • Q9: What if I get a network issue while having a conversation?

    If you get a network issue while having a conversation you will get a pop message that you lost connection, Click “OK” to refresh the page.



Avatar Interaction Instructions

    1. - Use the microphone button once or press the spacebar button once to speak and ask questions to Hannah.
    2. - Use the chat box to interact with Hannah by typing your questions to ask.
    3. - If you would like to provide feedback, click on the feedback button. Enter your feedback and submit.
    4. - To get information regarding the interaction with Hannah, you can click on the info button.
    5. - Use the CC button to turn on/off User and Hannah captions or, you can also click on the microphone button and give verbal commands like "Turn on my subtitles" or "Turn off your subtitles."
    6. - If you need to interrupt Hannah while she is speaking, you can click on the "Pause" button.
    7. - If you want to go to the dashboard page, simply click on the right-hand side minimize icon or you can click on the microphone button and ask Hannah to "Open Dashboard”.

  • Note: In Avatar Interaction Page do not use Verbal Commands like:
    1. - Next.
    2. - Previous.
    3. - Repeat
    4. - Stop/Close.

    1. - Go to the Learning Module.
    2. - Choose the module.
    3. - Click on Start.
